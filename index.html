WHAT IS HCI ?

About


Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) is a multidisciplinary field that focuses on the design and study of how people interact with computers and digital systems. Its primary goal is to create technology that is not only functional but also intuitive, efficient, and satisfying for users. HCI integrates knowledge from computer science, psychology, design, and ergonomics to understand user needs and behaviors.


TYPES OF HCI

COMMAND LINE INTERFACE(CLI)
GRAPHICAL USER INTERFACE(GUI)
MENU-BASED INTERFACE
FORM BASED INTERFACE
